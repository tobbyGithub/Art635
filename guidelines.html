Requirements/ Guidelines for
Online Competition & Workshop Application

  • All Applicants must apply for the Art635 gallery and be successfully added to the gallery (if they are not already artists in the gallery) to participate in the competition. Click here to register to join the Art635 gallery if you have not.
  • Each applicant for the competition upload 3 photographs on the theme “the elegance of the African people”.
  • All photographs must have a resolution of 72 dpi with dimension not larger than 1200 X 1800 pixels.
  • Each work must be labelled with the artist’s name, date, title and Days of Dorcas. (e.g. obu_peter_2_8_2017_myface_kitten_DaysofDorcas).
  • All materials (texts and photographs) should be added to the application form for the competition.

Start Application
    • No entry fee is required to apply and participate.
    • Workshop participants will be selected from the top 25 entries of the online competition.
    • Online Competition is open to photographers across Africa that will be able to attend the workshop in Lagos, Nigeria from Monday the 28th of May 2018 to Saturday the 2nd June 2018. Upon acceptance to attend the workshop, GTBank and Art635 will not be responsible for the lodging and transportation of participants before, during and after the workshop.
  • Shortlisted workshop participants will receive an information PDF pack in their confirmation email.
  • Incomplete applications/submissions that do not meet the aforementioned requirements and conditions will not be accepted.
